The Concert

2014

This historic 4-6 hour concert event will feature a minimum of 10 national artists on stage and will be simulcast worldwide. This event plans to be more collaborative than any concert has ever been before.

WHERE: Meadowlands (New York/New Jersey)

WHEN: December 5th, 2014

WHY: To support the creation of the Nelson Mandela Children’s Hospital.

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS

This live event will feature broadcast and streaming opportunities with artist performances and live content from two continents.

Interspersed within the performances, will be documentary vignettes on the life and legacy of Nelson Mandela, as well as, celebrity testimonials and impact statements surrounding the value of this much needed facility.

A documentary featuring the “making of” and “behind the scenes” account as well as, the actual concert itself, will be made and sold in support of the hospital.

Fundraising events and media opportunities will be planned before, during and after the event.
